# Your Oak Park Tax Dollar

An interactive breakdown of how your property tax bill is allocated across Village of Oak Park services and obligations.

## What it does

Enter your annual property tax bill and see exactly how much goes to each Village department — from Police and Fire operations to Public Works, Administration, and pensions. The tool visualizes the Village's 8.5% general levy (split across 10 departments) and the separate 6.7% pension levy.

**Features:**
- Adjust your tax bill amount to see personalized dollar breakdowns
- Toggle between annual and monthly views
- Click any bar to exclude a department and see updated totals
- Expand department details to see what services your taxes fund
- Fully mobile-responsive

## Data source

Based on the Village of Oak Park FY2025 adopted budget. Department percentage allocations reflect the general fund operating budget.

## Hosting

The page is a single self-contained `index.html` file with no external dependencies — all CSS and JavaScript are embedded. It can be served directly via GitHub Pages or any static file host.
